## My Process

1. **Discovery:** We discuss your project goals, target audience, tone, and specifications
2. **Research:** I conduct thorough research to ensure accuracy and depth
3. **Draft:** I create an initial draft for your review
4. **Revision:** We refine the content based on your feedback (typically 2-3 rounds included)
5. **Delivery:** Final polished content delivered in your preferred format
